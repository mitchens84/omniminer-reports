4. **Task risk gradient guides review intensity** — highest risk for numerical synthesis and regulatory language, lowest for formatting and layout.<sup>[4]</sup>

6. **Iterative Ralph loop yields A‑level work** — alternating generation (Codex) and review (Claude Opus 4.7) produces polished, accurate artifacts with minimal manual effort.<sup>[6]</sup>
7. **Domain expertise remains indispensable** — AI amplifies but does not replace deep knowledge; practitioners must curate and validate the source packet.<sup>[7]</sup>
